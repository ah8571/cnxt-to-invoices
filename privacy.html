Privacy policy

How cnxt to invoices currently handles browser data, account-linked invoice data, and product updates.

Overview

cnxt to invoices is designed to let you create invoices quickly with minimal friction. Some invoice data can live only in your browser, and some data can be stored in connected services when account features are enabled.

Browser-stored data

When you use the invoice maker without signing in, invoice drafts, invoice numbers, and related editing state may be stored locally in your browser so your work is not lost when you refresh or return later.

Account-linked data

When connected account features are enabled, business details, client records, saved drafts, and invoice records may be stored in third-party infrastructure used to run the product, including services such as Supabase.

Third-party services

cnxt to invoices may rely on external providers for authentication, data storage, hosting, and related product operations. Those providers may process data needed to deliver the service.

Your responsibility

You are responsible for reviewing invoice content before sending, printing, or sharing it. Avoid entering sensitive information you do not want stored in your browser or connected account systems.

Policy updates

This policy is an early production version and will be expanded over time with more specific details about retention, analytics, support contact, and data handling practices.
